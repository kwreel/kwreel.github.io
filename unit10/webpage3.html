All-purpose Flour 2 1/4 cups
Baking Soda 1 teaspoon
Salt 1 teaspoon
Butter 1 cup
Sugar 3/4 cup
Brown Sugar 3/4 cup
Vanilla Extract 1 teaspoon
Egg 2
Semi-sweet Chocolate Morsels 2 cups
Preheat oven to 375Â° F. Combine flour, baking soda and salt in small bowl. Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar and vanilla extract in large mixer bowl until creamy. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Gradually beat in flour mixture. Stir in morsels. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until golden brown. Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool completely.
